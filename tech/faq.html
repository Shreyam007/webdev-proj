Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Study Buddy Finder

General Questions

What is Study Buddy Finder?

Study Buddy Finder is a platform that helps students connect with compatible study partners based on their subjects, schedules, learning styles, and preferences. Our smart matching algorithm suggests the best study buddies to help you achieve your academic goals.

How does Study Buddy Finder work?

After creating your profile and specifying your study preferences, our algorithm will suggest potential study buddies. You can browse profiles, send connection requests, and once matched, coordinate study sessions through our messaging system.

Is Study Buddy Finder free to use?

Yes! The basic features of Study Buddy Finder are completely free to use. We also offer a Premium membership with additional features like advanced matching filters and priority support.

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

You can sign up using your email address or through your university email if available. Simply click on "Sign Up" and follow the instructions to create your profile.

Can I change my study preferences after signing up?

Absolutely! You can update your subjects, availability, and study preferences at any time in your profile settings. These changes will be reflected in your future matches.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account in the Account Settings section. Please note this action is permanent and will remove all your data from our system.

Matching & Connections

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our algorithm considers multiple factors including your subjects, study schedule, preferred study locations (in-person or online), learning style, and academic goals to find the most compatible study partners.

Can I search for study buddies myself?

Yes! While we provide smart matches, you can also browse and search for study buddies based on specific criteria like subject, university, or study method.

What should I do if I'm not getting good matches?

Try updating your profile with more detailed information about your study preferences. The more specific you are, the better our algorithm can match you. You can also expand your search criteria.

Safety & Privacy

Is my personal information safe?

We take your privacy very seriously. Your personal information is protected and never shared without your consent. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

What safety features are in place for in-person study sessions?

We recommend always meeting in public spaces like libraries or campus study areas. You can share your meeting plans with friends, and our platform allows you to report any concerning behavior.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

Every profile has a "Report" button. You can also email our support team with details. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

Premium Features

What are the benefits of Premium membership?

Premium members get access to advanced matching filters, unlimited messaging, priority customer support, and the ability to see who viewed their profile.

How much does Premium cost?

We offer monthly and yearly subscription plans. Pricing varies by region, but we always offer student discounts. Check the Premium page in your account for current pricing.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel at any time. Your Premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Still have questions?

Our support team is happy to help with any other questions you might have about Study Buddy Finder.
